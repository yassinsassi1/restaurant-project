Restaurant Menu

Appetizers

  • Caprese Skewers

    Small skewers of fresh mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, and basil, drizzled with balsamic glaze. Simple, fresh, and delicious!.


  • Stuffed Mushrooms

    • Mushroom caps filled with a mixture of cream cheese, garlic, spinach, and Parmesan cheese, then baked until golden.


  • Guacamole and Chips

    • Creamy guacamole made with mashed avocado, lime, cilantro, onion, and a hint of jalapeño, served with crispy tortilla chips.


  • Bruschetta

    • Toasted baguette slices topped with a mix of diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, and a sprinkle of balsamic vinegar

Main Courses

  • Grilled Chicken Parmesan

    Breaded and fried chicken cutlets, topped with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese, served over spaghetti or your favorite pasta.


  • Beef Stir-Fry

    • Thinly sliced beef sautéed with bell peppers, broccoli, onions, and a savory stir-fry sauce made from soy sauce, garlic, and ginger, served over steamed rice.

  • Vegetable Lasagna

    • Layers of pasta, ricotta cheese, spinach, mushrooms, zucchini, and marinara sauce, baked until bubbly and golden.

  • Salmon with Lemon-Dill Sauce

    • Pan-seared or baked salmon fillets topped with a tangy lemon-dill sauce, served with roasted vegetables or a fresh green salad.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Lava Cakes

    Rich, individual chocolate cakes with a gooey, molten center, served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.


  • Tiramisu

    • A classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cream, and a dusting of cocoa powder.


  • Lemon Meringue Pie

    • A tangy lemon custard filling topped with a fluffy, golden meringue on a flaky pie crust, baked to perfection.


  • Berry Parfait

    • Layers of fresh mixed berries, sweetened whipped cream or Greek yogurt, and granola, served in individual glasses for a light, refreshing treat.
